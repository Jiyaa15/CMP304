Ingredients:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup blueberries
    Directions:
  1. Mix flour and sugar together
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together milk and egg
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients together
  4. Mix in blueberrries
  5. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 17 minimute
Notes:
For Lemone Blueberry Muffins, add 1 tbsp lemone zest
